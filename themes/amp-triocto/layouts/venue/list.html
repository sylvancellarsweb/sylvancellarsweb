Craft Tasting Room

The much-anticipated Tasting Room opened at Sylvan Cellars in January 2016 featuring a vast selection of handcrafted cocktails, craft beer, and a wine list from all over the globe. Wine, beer, and cocktail enthusiasts alike can choose from 18 craft beers on tap, select craft beer bottles, a wide selection of craft cocktails, and a worldly collection of wines. Don’t forget about domestic beer and well drinks, we simply provide something for all different palates. Specialty cocktails are available like our lovely, local Lavender Lane cocktail, as well as our own twist to the worldwide Moscow Mule, and so much more!

Keep up to date on our schedule of Live Entertainment throughout the year! Come on out and enjoy a drink you can’t get anywhere else in an atmosphere you will never forget.

Tasting room hours are Monday through Thursday 2-9 p.m., Friday 2 to midnight, Saturday noon to midnight, and Sunday 11am to 4 p.m.
